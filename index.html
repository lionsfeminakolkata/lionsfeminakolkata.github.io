About Us

Lions Club Femina Kolkata is a branch of the international service organization called Lions Clubs International (LCI) based in Kolkata, India. Lions Clubs International is the largest service club organization in the world, with a mission to empower volunteers to serve their communities, meet humanitarian needs, and promote peace and understanding worldwide. Lions Club Femina Kolkata specifically focuses on addressing the needs and concerns of women in Kolkata and surrounding areas. It aims to bring together women from diverse backgrounds who are dedicated to serving their community and making a positive impact. The club organizes various service projects, fundraisers, and initiatives to support causes related to women's empowerment, education, healthcare, and social welfare. The members of Lions Club Femina Kolkata work collaboratively to identify local needs, develop projects, and implement solutions that aim to uplift and improve the lives of women and their communities. Through their collective efforts, they strive to create a more equitable and inclusive society where women can thrive. Lions Club Femina Kolkata is part of the larger Lions Clubs International network, which provides them with resources, support, and a global network of like-minded individuals working towards similar goals. By being part of this network, Lions Club Femina Kolkata has access to a vast range of service opportunities and collaborations on a regional, national, and international level. Overall, Lions Club Femina Kolkata serves as a platform for women in Kolkata to come together, contribute to meaningful service projects, and make a positive impact in their community, aligning with the broader mission of Lions Clubs International.
